## Health Benefits of Hiking and Trekking
1. It lowers the risk of heart disease and improves your blood pressure.
2. Better overall fitness.
3. Improves your mental health.
4. Burns calories; and
5. Makes you creative.

## Safety Reminders in Hiking and Trekking
1. **H**ike with a group and never alone. Hike within your skills. Hike within the trails and be aware of surroundings.
2. **I**nstructions from **guides**, **facilitators**, or **leader** should be given utmost attention. Rules and regulations must be followed strictly.
3. **K**eep in mind and heart the [[Resources-Data/Knowledge/Physical Education/Recreation/Outdoor Recreation/The Leave No Trace Seven Principles\|Leave No Trace Principles]].
4. **E**nsure safety of self and others. Learn to apply first aid. Learn where to go and what to do during emergency situations.
