A **business organization** refers to the ==legal structure== under which a business operates.

** **

+----------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------+
| A. **Sole Proprietorship**                   | * Owned and managed by ==one person==.                |
|                                              | * ==Easy to establish== and manage.                   |
|                                              | * Owner has ==unlimited liability==.                  |
+----------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------+
| B. **Partnership**                           | * Owned by ==two or more persons==.                   |
|                                              | * Profits and responsibilities are shared.            |
|                                              | * Can be general or limited partnership.              |
+----------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------+
| C. **Corporation**                           | * Owned by ==shareholders==.                          |
|                                              | * Has a separate legal personality.                   |
|                                              | * Owners have ==limited liability==.                  |
+----------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------+
| D. **Cooperative**                           | * Owned and operated by ==members==.                  |
|                                              | * Formed to ==meet common economic or social needs==. |
+----------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------+
| E. **Other Forms of Business Organizations** | * **Franchise**                                       |
|                                              | * **Joint Venture**                                   |
|                                              | * Non-stock and Non-profit Organizations              |
+----------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------+
