## A. Local Business Environment
Factors ==within the country== that affect business operations.

* local economic conditions;
* government policies and regulations;
* labor market and workforce skills;
* local suppliers and competitors; and
* cultural practices and consumer preferences.

## B. International Business Environment
Factors ==outside the country== that influence ==firms engaged in global operations==.

* global economic conditions;
* international trade agreements;
* foreign exchange rates;
* cultural differences;
* political stability of other countries; and
* international laws and regulations

** **
Understanding both local and international environments helps firms ==compete effectively== in the global market.
