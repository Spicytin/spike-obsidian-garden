**Economic development** refers to the process by which a country improves the **economic**, **political**, and **social well-being** of its people.

** **

+-------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1. **Traditional Society**          | * **Economy** is based on ==agriculture and subsistence farming==. |
|                                     | * Limited technology and low productivity.                         |
+-------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 2. **Preconditions for Take-Off**   | * Development of infrastructure and industries.                    |
|                                     | * Increased investments and savings.                               |
+-------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 3. **Take-Off Stage**               | * Rapid industrial growth.                                         |
|                                     | * Expansion of manufacturing and technology.                       |
+-------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 4. **Drive to Maturity**            | * Diversification of industries.                                   |
|                                     | * Adoption of advanced technology.                                 |
+-------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 5. **Age of High Mass Consumption** | * Focus on consumer goods and services.                            |
|                                     | * Higher standard of living.                                       |
+-------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
