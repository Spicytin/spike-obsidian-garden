## A. Environmental Forces
External factors that ==influence the operations and performance of an organization==. These forces are typically beyond the firm's control, but must still be monitored and addressed.

### Major Environmental Forces

+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1. **Economic Forces**                     | Includes **inflation**, **interest rates**, **unemployment**, **income levels**, and **economic growth**, which directly ==affect consumer purchasing power and business profitability==. |
+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 2. **Political and Legal Forces**          | Involves **government policies**, **laws**, **regulations**, and **political stability** that ==affect business operations==.                                                             |
+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 3. **Socio-Cultural Forces**               | Includes **beliefs**, **values**, **customs**, **lifestyle trends**, and **population demographics** that ==influence consumer behavior==                                                 |
+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 4. **Technological Forces**                | ==Innovations and technological changes== that affect **production methods**, **communication**, and **product development**.                                                             |
+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 5. **Competitive Forces**                  | Includes **competitors**, **market rivalry**, and **industry competition** that ==affect a firm's position== in the market.                                                               |
+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 6. **Environmental and Ecological Forces** | Involves concerns about **sustainability**, **climate change**, and **environmental protection**.                                                                                         |
+--------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

## B. Environmental Scanning
The process of **collecting**, **analyzing**, and **interpreting information** about external environmental forces to ==identify opportunities and threats==.

### Importance of Environmental Scanning
* helps managers anticipate changes;
* identifies potential risks and opportunities;
* improves strategic planning and decision-making; and
* enhances organizational adaptability.
