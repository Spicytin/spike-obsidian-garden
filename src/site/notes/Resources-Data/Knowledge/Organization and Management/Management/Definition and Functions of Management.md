The process of **planning**, **organizing**, **leading**, and **controlling** resources to achieve specific goals efficiently and effectively. It involves ==coordinating people and resources== to accomplish desired objectives. 

According to ***Mary Parker Folett***, management is "*the art of getting things done through people*."

## Importance of Management
* helps organizations achieve goals;
* ensures efficient use of resources;
* maintains order and coordination;
* improves productivity; and
* adapts the organization to changes in the environment.

## Functions of Management

+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1. **Planning**    | Setting goals and determining the best course of action to achieve them. It includes **forecasting**, **setting objectives**, and **decision-making**.                         |
+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 2. **Organizing**  | Arranging **tasks**, **people**, and **resources** in a structured manner. It includes assigning duties, grouping tasks, and establishing authority relationships.             |
+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 3. **Leading**     | Involves **motivating**, **guiding**, and **supervising** employees to ==accomplish organizational goals==. It includes **communication**, **leadership**, and **motivation**. |
+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 4. **Controlling** | Process of monitoring performance, comparing it with standards, and taking corrective action when necessary to ensure goals are met.                                           |
+--------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
