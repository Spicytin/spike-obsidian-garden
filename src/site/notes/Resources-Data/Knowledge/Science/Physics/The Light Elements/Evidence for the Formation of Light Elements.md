The light elements were cooked up shortly after the big bang. Scientists have confirmed this by studying:

+-----------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1. **Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation** (***CMBR***) | Discovered by ***Arno Penzias*** and ***Robert Wilson***, it is the ==leftover heat== from the early universe. This radiation is the "*==fingerprint==*" of the **Big Bang**.              |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 2. **Abundance of Light Elements**                        | The amount of ***hydrogen*** and ***helium*** in space today matches amounts predicted by **Big Bang** calculations. This exact ratio is strong evidence that they were formed very early. |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 3. **Redshift of Galaxies**                               | ***Edwin Hubble*** discovered that galaxies are ==moving away from us==. This supports an ==expanding universe== that began from one point.                                                |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
