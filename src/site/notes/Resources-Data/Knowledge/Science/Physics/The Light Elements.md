+----------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ***Hydrogen*** | The first and ==most abundant element== in the universe. About ==75% of all matter== in the universe today is still hydrogen.                                               |
+----------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ***Helium***   | Formed when two ***[[Resources-Data/Knowledge/Science/Chemistry/Atoms#Main Parts of an Atom\|protons]]*** and ***[[Resources-Data/Knowledge/Science/Chemistry/Atoms#Main Parts of an Atom\|neutrons]]*** combined. It makes up ==about 25%== of the early universe. |
+----------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ***Lithium***  | Produced only in small amounts and is the ==third lightest element== created.                                                                                               |
+----------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+


These elements matter because without them, the stars would not form, planets and heavier elements would not exist; and life would not be developed. The light elements became the ==building blocks== of the entire universe.
