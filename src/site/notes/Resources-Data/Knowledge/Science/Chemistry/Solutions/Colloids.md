A type of mixture where very small particles are ==evenly dispersed== in another substance but are not completely dissolved.

## Characteristics of Colloids
* Particles are larger than those in solutions but smaller than those in suspensions.
* Do not settle when left undisturbed.
* Scatter light (Tyndall effect).

## Examples
* Milk
* Fog
* Gel
* Whipped cream
