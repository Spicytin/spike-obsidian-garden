## Solution
A homogeneous mixture in which one substance is ==evenly dissolved in another==. 

> [!note]
> Homogeneous means that the mixture has a ==uniform composition== throughout.

### Examples
* Salt dissolved in water
* Sugar dissolved in water
* Air (mixture of gases)

## Solute
The substance that is ==being dissolved==. It is usually present in a ==smaller amount==.

### Examples
* Salt in salt water
* Sugar in sugar solution

## Solvent
The substance that ==dissolves the solute==. It is usually present in a ==larger amount==.

### Examples
* Water in salt water
* Water in sugar solution

> [!note]
> Water is called the universal solvent because it can ==dissolve many substances==.
