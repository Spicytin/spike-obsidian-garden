## Acids
Substances that release ***hydrogen*** ions ($H^+$) when dissolved in water.

### Common Properties of Acids
* Sour taste
* React with metals
* Turn blue litmus paper into red

### Examples
* ***Hydrochloric*** acid ($HCl$)
* ***Sulfuric*** acid () $H_2SO_4$)
* ***Vinegar*** (acetic acid)

## Bases
Substances that release ***hydroxide*** ions ($OH^-$) when dissolved in water.

### Common Properties of Bases
* Bitter taste
* Slippery feel
* Turn red litmus paper into blue

### Examples
* ***Sodium hydroxide*** ($NaOH$)
* ***Calcium hydroxide*** ($Ca(OH)_2$)
* Soap
