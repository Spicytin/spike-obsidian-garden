The study of [[Resources-Data/Knowledge/Science/Chemistry/Acids and Bases\|acids and bases]] developed over time as scientists tried to explain their properties.

+-------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1. **Early Observations**     | Acids were known for their sour taste and ability to corrode metals. Bases were known for their bitter taste and slippery feel.                                                   |
+-------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 2. **Arrhenius Theory**       | ***Svante Arrhenius*** defined acids as substances that produce ***hydrogen*** ions ($H^+$) in water and bases as substances that produce ***hydroxide*** ions ($OH^-$) in water. |
+-------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 3.  **Br√∏nsted-Lowry Theory** | Acids are proton donors, while bases are proton acceptors.                                                                                                                        |
+-------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 4. **Lewis Theory**           | Acids accept electron pairs, while bases donate electron pairs.                                                                                                                   |
+-------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
