## Structures of Order

+----------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Community**  | One of the social order which refers to the ==association of individual== who shares a common identity.          |
+----------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Government** | The higher level of social order that exist ==primarily for the maintenance== and perpetuation of the community. |
+----------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **State**      | The largest order today and in which the term politics originally derived.                                       |
