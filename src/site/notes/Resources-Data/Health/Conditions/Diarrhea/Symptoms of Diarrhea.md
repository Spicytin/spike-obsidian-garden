## Acute Watery (*Cholera*)
* Weight loss
* **Dehydration**

## Bloody Diarrhea (*Dysentery*)
* Intestinal damage
* **Sepsis** (severe infection)
* **Dehydration**

## Persistent Diarrhea
* **Malnutrition**
* **Dehydration**
* Serious intestinal infection

## Diarrhea with severe Malnutrition
* Heart failure
* Damage to many internal organs
* Vitamin & mineral deficiencies
