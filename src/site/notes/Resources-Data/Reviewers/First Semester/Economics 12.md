1. **Business** - undertaking by a person, or a group of persons in partnership, or stockholders (who known a legal entity known as a **corporation**) for the main purpose of ==earning profit==.
2. **Industry** - specific market of a ==certain competitive structure==.
3. ***SWOT analysis*** - tool that ==evaluates the competitive position of a business==.
4. **Sole proprietorship** - form of **business organization** owned by an individual who is ==singly responsible for running the business==.
5. **Partnership** - form of **business organization** in which ==two or more persons combine their resources== in a business.
6. **Corporation** - legal entity owned by a ==group of persons== and other entities as **shareholders** who are not ==personally liable==.
7. **Cooperative** - entity organized by people with similar needs to ==provide themselves with goods or services==.
8. ***Porter's Five forces of Competitive Position*** - simple framework to assess and ==evaluate the competitive position== of a business.
9. **Circular Flow of Economic Activity** - neoclassical economic model ==depicting how money and output flow==.
10. **Household** - consists of persons as the ==basic consuming unit of goods and services==.
11. **Business firm** - ==delivers the goods and services== to the household for its consumption.

## Flows

| **Flows**          | **Meaning**                     |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------- |
| **Physical flow**  | Flow of ==goods and resources== |
| **Financial flow** | Flow of ==money payments==      |

## Goods

| **Goods**              | **Meaning**                  |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Raw materials**      | Unprocessed goods            |
| **Intermediate goods** | Semi-processed goods         |
| **Final goods**        | Ready for direct consumption |

## Analysis

| **Analysis**               | **Meaning**                                                         |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Industry analysis**      | Studies the ==conditions in an industry==.                          |
| **Environmental analysis** | Evaluation of the possible/probable effects of **external forces**. |

## Sectors

| **Sector**                                            | **Meaning**                                                                                                     |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Agriculture**, **fishery**, and **forestry sector** | Reaps the fruits of ==natural resources==.                                                                      |
| **Industrial sector**                                 | Processes ==raw materials== from **agriculture**, **fishery**, and **forestry** into ==intermediate products==. |
| **Service sector**                                    | Produces the intangibles ==supporting and complementing production== in other sectors.                          |
